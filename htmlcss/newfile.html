How does Odoo Generate Revenue?

Enterprise Purchases
Odoo has an Enterprise Edition with a ton of extra features which has a very affordable Subscription cost.

The Hours spent by the QuickStart Team and the Hours Spent by the Offshore team to customize Odoo are billabe.
Partner Commission
Odoo can be sold by Partners all over the world. Odoo takes a cut of these sales generated by the Partners.
